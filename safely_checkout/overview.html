Safely Checkout Overview

Overview​

The Safely Finance checkout flow begins when a tenant selects Safely Finance as a payment option, and ends when the tenant receives and selects an offer, or closes the checkout flow.

1. Tenant selects Safely Finance checkout​

On your website, you can offer Safely Checkout as a payment option. If the tenant selects this option, they'll be presented with a pop-up to start the Safely Finance checkout flow.

2. Supplies information for their offers​

The tenant has the opportunity to request an additional amount to borrow and verifies their address.

3. Receives instant offers​

Offers are instantaneously checked and presented to the tenant.

4. Tenant eligibility is confirmed​

The tenant is returned to the original website where Safely Finance is integrated.
