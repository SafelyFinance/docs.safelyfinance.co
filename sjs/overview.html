Safely.js reference

This guide explains the safely.js browser-side Javascript library.

Safely.js is used to integrate the variety of payment options Safely Finance makes available to your customers at checkout. This may be Google Pay, Apple Pay, credit or debit cards, pay-over-time or bank transfers.

Overview​

The Safely Finance checkout flow begins when a customer selects Safely Finance as a payment option, and ends when the customer receives and selects an offer, or closes the checkout flow.

1. Customer selects Safely Finance checkout​

On your website, you can offer Safely Checkout payment options. If the customer selects an option presented by Safely Finance, they'll be presented with a pop-up to start the checkout flow.

2. Customer provides information to determine their offers​

The customer has the opportunity to request an additional amount to borrow and verifies their address.

3. Instant offers are displayed​

Eligibility is instantaneously checked and offers presented to the customer.

4. Customer completes their checkout​

The customer is returned to your website to complete the checkout process.
